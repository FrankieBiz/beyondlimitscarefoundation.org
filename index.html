Our Programs

Equine Therapy

Therapeutic riding sessions led by certified autism-trained instructors that help children develop physical, emotional, and cognitive skills.

Village Farm Program

Animal interaction with goats, sheep, bunnies, ducks, and horses at The Village Farm (2020 Woodbourne Road, Langhorne, PA).

Community Partnerships

Fully funded programs for local families who couldn't otherwise afford these life-changing therapeutic experiences through community support and partnerships.

How We Help

Emotional

Reducing stress & anxiety while building confidence and emotional regulation

Cognitive

Improving focus, problem-solving skills, and sequential thinking

Physical

Developing balance, coordination, muscle tone and motor skills

Social

Enhancing communication, trust, empathy and teamwork abilities
